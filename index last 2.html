Interests

Seeking the naturally occurring, insightful mindsets, emotional contexts, intuitive and random points of view, timeless aesthetics.

vision

True artistry thrives on pushing boundaries, venturing into the realm of the futuristic. This isn't just about technology; it's about the spirit of exploration, the endless passion that fuels our ambition to translate our reality through the power of film, music and digital media.

story

Raised by American expats in Denmark sparked a love for the arts. First with a dusty upright gifted by departing neighbors, and later through the energy of teenage bands in the suburbs. Became a music store apprentice surrounded by sonic story telling tools, then navigated the complex business of audio post-production at a busy startup in Copenhagen. Moved to California to do cutting edge sound design. Since then traveling the world as a digital nomad incorporating filmmaking as a serious side hustle, while also dabbling in academics and interactive media.

future

It has been a privilege to collaborate with global talents, witnessing firsthand the magic that unfolds when diverse skills converge. While the luxury of leisure has allowed the family to grow, the hopes of future generations fan the flames of imagination, as we discover entirely different ways to express our needs and desires, at speeds that exceeds even the blink of an eye.
