Story

It was an idyllic yet isolated world growing up with American parents in Denmark in the '60s and '70s. A desire for connection resonated with the music. First from a dusty upright, gifted by departing neighbors, and later through the energy of teenage bands in the suburbs. Found a job in a music store in the '80s surrounded by sonic storytelling tools, then navigated the complex business of audio post-production at a busy startup in Copenhagen. Moved to California in the '90s to do cutting edge sound design at an award winning studio in Venice Beach. Then became an independent entrepreneur through various business ventures and partnerships.

Work

It has been a privilege to collaborate with global talents, witnessing firsthand the magic that unfolds when diverse skills converge. Embracing the most recent recording technology, enabling to capture the essence of sound and image whenever inspiration strikes, whether powered by solar panels or a humble wall outlet. Everything is meticulously produced, performed, and programmed using a fusion of techniques, always with an explorative ambition to evoke cinematic atmospheres, thus creating unique and unheard electronic styles through random discovery. Whether wielding a battered upright piano or a cutting-edge modular synth, time melts away in this creative crucible. Here, the dream is to capture the emotions and desires of this wondrous life, weaving them into stories that ignite hearts and minds.
