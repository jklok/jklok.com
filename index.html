Story

It was an idyllic yet isolated world growing up with American parents in Denmark in the '60s and '70s. A desire for connection resonated with the music. First from a dusty upright, gifted by departing neighbors, and later through the energy of teenage bands in the suburbs. Found a job in a music store in the 80s surrounded by music instruments and recording equipment, then navigated the complex business of audio post-production at a busy startup. Moved to California in the 90s to do cutting edge sound design at an award winning studio. Became an independent entrepreneur through various business ventures and partnerships. Then decided to sell everything and live out of a suitcase traveling the world. Finding solitude in Southeast Asia, where the heat and energy combined with personal liberties inspired the creation of a wide variety of conceptual and creative outlets.

Interests

Seeking the naturally occurring, insightful mindsets. emotional contexts, intuitive and random points of view, timeless aesthetics. Inspired by a life that whispers rather than shouts. A conquest of tranquility, simplicity. A hunt for wisdom in the echoing halls of time, where truths lie hidden in the hum of the ages. An ode to the simple life, the unfettered spirit, and the boundless joy of staying alive. In the quietude of contemplation, in the embrace of nature's bounty, the truest measure of success is found, the richest prize of all – a life lived with eyes wide open, heart ablaze, and soul at peace. From the boundless banquet of knowledge spread before us all. Millennia of minds laid bare, a testament to the flickering flame of human consciousness. In their words, finding solace, in their struggles, strength, in their triumphs, echoes of own yearning. As psychology and sociology map the landscapes of the human heart, historians weave tapestries of empires risen and fallen, scientists unveil the universe's grand design, and artists paint symphonies with light and clay. The eternal experience of bare existence becomes the source of energy that animates all ambition.
